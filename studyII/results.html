Study II: Results

This page presents the results obtained from the second survey.
In the Best Recommendation tab you will find the best suggestion (from participants' view) among the ones proposed for each task. You can also see the motivation provided by the participants for their selection.
In the Proposal Evaluation tab you can find a sample of the evaluation of the recommendations for four of the characteristics we asked participants to asses (you can download the complete file).
Best Recommendation Proposal Evaluation
This table reports, for each task, the best recommendation chosen by participants (among the 4 proposed) and their assessment of its usefulness (Slightly Useful, Useful, Very Useful).

The last column, None Analysis, presents our interpretation of the participants' explanations for choosing "None of the proposed recommendations are useful".

In this table we report a sample of the evaluation performed by participants on some characteristics of two recommendations (the one selected as useful recommendation, and another random one). The column "Is useful" will be "True" if the evaluation relates to a recommendation considered as useful.

Note that the table only reports 4 out of 15 characteristics evaluated by participants. The full data is available for download via the "Download table" button below.
